Our Mission 05-Feb-2020

Our goal is to provide you the knowledge you need to comprehend and influence the world. In order for you to fully comprehend the difficulties we face, potential solutions, and what you can do, we lead you on a journey from curiosity to comprehension by providing context and clarity to the events and concerns whirling around you.

Talha Khan

Founder

Facebook Twitter Instagram

He has been a political and educational activist for more than 5 years. According to Talha, unbiased media has the power to change society. Our progress requires an understanding of current events through the eyes of millennials from North America to Asia, and exhibiting them on a digital platform where the audience may learn from one another. GOL is set up to play a role in that shared evolution process.
